**Post 2 - How Economies Actually Work**
```
HOOK: "Quick economics lesson that changes everything."

BODY: "The economy is not a fixed pie. When people have money, they spend
it. That spending creates demand. Demand creates jobs. Jobs create income.
Income creates spending.

It's a cycle, not a static pool.

Citizen's Royalty doesn't take money out of the economy. It moves money to people who
will spend it. Consumer spending is 70% of GDP. More spending = more
economy.

The fear that 'we can't afford it' misunderstands how money works."

### Day 81

**Post 1 - Inflation Deep Dive**
```
HOOK: "Will Citizen's Royalty cause inflation? Let's really break this down."

BODY: "Inflation happens when too much money chases too few goods.

Citizen's Royalty doesn't print new money. It's funded by taxes. Money moves from one
pocket to another. Total money supply doesn't change.

Could some prices rise? Maybe at the bottom - lowest rents, lowest wages.

But economy-wide inflation? The evidence says no. Alaska has had dividends
for 40 years. No unusual inflation. Stimulus checks didn't cause lasting
inflation (supply chains did).

The theory says no. The evidence says no."

**Post 2 - Automation Tax Mechanics**
```
HOOK: "How does an automation tax actually work? Let me explain."

BODY: "The automation tax measures productivity gains - output per worker
hour. When that goes up due to automation, we tax a portion.

Simple example:
- Factory produced 100 widgets with 10 workers = 10/worker
- Now produces 100 widgets with 5 workers = 20/worker
- Productivity doubled. We tax 15% of that gain.

Not taxing revenue. Not taxing profit. Taxing the productivity increase
that came from replacing workers with machines.

The company still keeps 85% of the gain. But society shares in the benefit."

**Post 2 - International Competitiveness**
```
HOOK: "How do we stay competitive globally with these policies?"

BODY: "The VAT is the answer.

Every other developed country has a VAT. They refund it on exports and
apply it to imports. This is WTO-legal.

With a VAT:
- US exports don't carry the tax (refunded at border)
- Imports DO pay the tax (applied at entry)
- Level playing field

The automation tax works similarly. Border adjustment means foreign
producers pay when selling here.

We don't disadvantage American companies. We level the field."

CTA: "Level playing field. Not handicap."

HASHTAGS: #Competitiveness #VAT #Trade #BorderAdjustment
