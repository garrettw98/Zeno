## Introduction

The Human Standard is not a utopian fantasy to be achieved overnight. It is a thirty-year transition from the world we have to the world we want. This document maps that journey phase by phase, milestone by milestone, decision by decision.

The transition has three phases:

| Phase | Years | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Foundation** | 2025-2030 | Build infrastructure, run pilots, shift culture |
| **Implementation** | 2030-2040 | Deploy systems, scale programs, prove models |
| **Transformation** | 2040-2055 | Complete transition, achieve full vision |

Each phase builds on the last. Skip steps, and the foundation crumbles. Rush the timeline, and backlash destroys progress. This is a generational project requiring generational patience but it starts now.

## Phase 2: Implementation (2030-2040)

### Objective

Move from pilot programs to policy implementation. Prove the models work at scale. Build systems that can handle full automation transition.

### The Automation Acceleration

By 2030, the automation crisis will be undeniable:
- Humanoid robots in mass production (millions of units)
- AI handling most routine cognitive work
- Autonomous vehicles dominant in logistics
- White-collar displacement accelerating

The question will no longer be IF but HOW FAST and HOW DO WE RESPOND.

### Policy Implementation Timeline

#### 2030-2032: Automation Productivity Tax

**Year 1: Federal Disclosure Mandate**
- All companies over 500 employees report automation metrics
- Standardized measurement methodology
- Public database of automation by industry and region
- Early warning system for displacement hotspots

**Year 2: Tax Introduction**
- 5% Automation Productivity Tax on measured value
- Corresponding 5% reduction in employer payroll taxes
- Small business exemptions (under 100 employees)
- Revenue directed to Automation Transition Fund

**Year 3: Rate Adjustment**
- Increase to 10% APT based on measured impact
- Further payroll tax reductions
- Transition Fund reaching significant scale
- First dividend payments to most-affected regions

#### 2032-2035: Citizen's Royalty Scaling

**From Pilots to Programs:**
- Successful state pilots expanded
- Federal matching funds for state programs
- Standardized enrollment and payment systems
- Integration with existing benefits

**Coverage Expansion:**
- 2032: Most-displaced workers and regions
- 2033: Expansion to poverty-level households
- 2034: Expansion to all below median income
- 2035: Universal adult coverage begins

**Payment Scaling:**
- Start at $500/month
- Increase to $750/month by 2034
- Reach Citizen's Royalty Phase 2 ($18k floor) by 2036
- Adjust based on automation dividend revenue

#### 2033-2037: Truth Infrastructure

**Blockchain Verification:**
- Government communications verified by default
- Media organizations adopting standards
- Consumer devices with authentication
- Legal evidentiary status for verified content

**Identity Systems:**
- Self-sovereign identity pilots
- Government integration begins
- Private sector adoption
- International interoperability

#### 2035-2040: AI Governance

**Tiered Transparency Framework:**
- Federal procurement requires open source AI
- Audit requirements for government algorithms
- Appeals processes established
- State adoption follows federal lead

**Algorithmic Administration:**
- Routine benefits processing automated
- Permit and licensing automation
- Tax filing fully automated
- Human oversight maintained for appeals

## Critical Path Dependencies

Some milestones must be achieved before others are possible:

```
2025-2027: Ideas spread, movement builds
    |
    v
2027-2030: Political legitimacy, pilot programs
    |
    v
2030-2033: Automation tax implemented, revenue generated
    |
    v
2033-2037: Citizen's Royalty scaled with automation revenue
    |
    v
2037-2045: Full economic security, work transformation
    |
    v
2045-2055: Global adoption, vision realized
```

**Blockers:**
- Cannot scale Citizen's Royalty without funding source (need automation tax first)
- Cannot implement automation tax without measurement (need disclosure first)
- Cannot achieve political change without cultural shift (need movement first)
- Cannot build movement without ideas (need framework first)

We are at the first step. Every subsequent step depends on it.

## Contingencies

### If Automation Is Slower Than Expected

**Scenario:** AGI takes until 2045+, displacement more gradual

**Response:**
- Extend Phase 1 and 2 timelines
- Build infrastructure anyway (valuable regardless)
- Use extra time for deeper cultural shift
- Policies still valuable for existing inequality

### If Automation Is Faster Than Expected

**Scenario:** AGI by 2030, massive displacement by 2035

**Response:**
- Accelerate Phase 2 dramatically
- Emergency Citizen's Royalty programs without full infrastructure
- Crisis measures for immediate displacement
- Catch up on infrastructure while providing relief

### If Political Opposition Succeeds

**Scenario:** Movement blocked, policies rejected

**Response:**
- Continue cultural and intellectual work
- Wait for crisis to change political calculus
- Support state-level implementation
- Build international pressure

### If Technology Goes Wrong

**Scenario:** AI safety failure, major accidents, public backlash

**Response:**
- Emphasize Human Standard safety provisions
- Human oversight, transparency, override requirements
- Position as SOLUTION to tech failures
- Accelerate transparency requirements

## Conclusion: The Thirty-Year Project

The Human Standard is not a campaign. It is not a policy proposal. It is not even a movement in the ordinary sense.

It is a thirty-year project to reshape the relationship between humanity and technology. To ensure that the most powerful tools ever created serve their creators rather than replace them. To build systems that distribute abundance rather than concentrate it.

This document maps the journey. The destination is a world where technology serves humanity, where automation frees rather than impoverishes, where truth is verifiable and governance is trustworthy.

We know how to get there. We have the ideas, the policies, the framework. What we need is time, effort, and will. Yours and millions of others.

The transition has begun. Every day that passes, automation advances. Every day we delay, the task grows harder. Every day we work, the vision comes closer.

Thirty years is both very long and very short. Long enough to build something real. Short enough that children born today will live in the world we create.

Build it well.
